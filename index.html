FAQ

How often is the data updated?
TrendRadar refreshes every 2 hours using the official YouTube Data API. You always see trends from the last 48 hours, sorted by current growth speed.
What is "velocity"?
Velocity is views-per-hour since publish, not total views. A video with 200K views in 6 hours scores higher than one with 1M views from last week — because velocity = opportunity.
How do I get my Pro key after paying?
After checkout on Gumroad you'll receive a TrendRadar access key by email within minutes. Enter it on the app's start screen to unlock all 9 markets and CSV export.
Can I use this for client work?
Yes. One Pro subscription covers your personal use and any client content strategies you run. There is no per-seat pricing at this time.
Does the free tier ever expire?
No. The US market is free indefinitely — no trial period, no credit card, no catch.
